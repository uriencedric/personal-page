Why I Decided To Create A Blog (Again)


Published on Thrusday, 30 March 20231 min read

Welcome to my blog! I'm thrilled to have you here and can't wait to share my thoughts, insights, and experiences with you. Whether you're a seasoned software developer or just starting your journey in the field, I hope you'll find something valuable and engaging in these pages.

As a software developer, I've spent countless hours writing code, debugging, testing, and deploying software. It's a challenging and rewarding profession that demands constant learning and adaptation to new technologies, frameworks, and methodologies. Over the years, I've learned a lot from my peers, mentors, and online resources, and I believe that sharing knowledge and experiences is essential for personal and professional growth.

That's why I started (or shall I say) this blog after a 10 years slumber due to a rookie mistake :( - to create a space where I can share my thoughts on various topics related to software development, including programming languages, tools, frameworks, design patterns, best practices, and more. I'll also cover broader topics that affect the software development industry, such as trends, challenges, ethics, security etc...

I'm passionate about writing code that is not only functional but also elegant, efficient, and maintainable. I believe that software development is both an art and a science, and that the best solutions come from a combination of creativity, logic, and collaboration. I'll share my tips and tricks on how to write clean, scalable, and robust code that solves real-world problems and delights users.


I hope you'll enjoy reading my posts as much as I enjoy writing them. Let's embark on this journey together !
